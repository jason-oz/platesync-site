# PlateSync — Explainer

## The Problem

Meal planning for a family is a weekly headache. Every person has different needs — dad's trying to hit his protein targets, mum's watching calories, the 6-year-old won't eat anything that touches on the plate, and the toddler needs completely different portions. Parents end up guessing quantities, buying too much, wasting food, and defaulting to the same five dinners on repeat.

Existing meal planning apps treat the whole family as one unit. They don't account for the fact that a 40-year-old man training five days a week needs a completely different plate than a 2-year-old.

## The Solution

PlateSync builds weekly meal plans where every person gets the right amount of food. It calculates portions based on each family member's age, weight, activity level, and goals — then tells you exactly how much to put on each plate. When you're done planning, it generates a shopping list that only includes what you actually need to buy.

## How It Works

1. **Set up your family** — Add each family member with their age, weight, height, activity level, fitness goals, allergies, and food preferences. Set their weekly training schedule so portions adjust on training vs rest days.

2. **Build your recipe collection** — Search the web for recipes using AI, or add your own. PlateSync catalogues each recipe with full ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and per-serving nutrition.

3. **Stock your supplies** — Log what you already have in the fridge, pantry, and freezer. Type or dictate a list and AI sorts it automatically. PlateSync uses this to rank recipes and subtract from your shopping list.

4. **Plan your week** — Assign recipes to each day. The app ranks recipes by how many ingredients you already have on hand and flags any allergy conflicts. Portions are calculated per person — the total never exceeds what the recipe actually makes.

5. **Confirm and shop** — Confirm the plan to generate a shopping list with full-batch ingredient amounts (because you cook the whole recipe). Pantry items are subtracted — you only buy what you need.

6. **Cook with confidence** — Tap any meal on your confirmed plan to open a mobile cooking guide: checkable ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and a clear portion breakdown per person. No ads, no life story — just the recipe.

7. **Handle leftovers** — After cooking, mark the meal as done. Surplus servings can be kept in the fridge, frozen, or discarded. Leftovers show up as an option for future meals — no extra shopping.

## Key Features

- **Per-person portions** — Science-backed calculations using Mifflin-St Jeor BMR, activity multipliers, and protein targets (0.9–2.2 g/kg depending on age, activity, and goals)
- **Day-aware nutrition** — Targets adjust per day based on each person's weekly training schedule and portion tendency
- **Allergy detection** — Automatic scanning against family members' allergies; smart enough to know "dairy-free milk" isn't dairy
- **Cooking mode** — Full-screen mobile view with checkable ingredients, step-by-step instructions, and per-person portion breakdown
- **Leftovers tracking** — Surplus servings tracked; assignable to future meals; fridge/freeze/discard management
- **Smart shopping lists** — Full-batch quantities, pantry deduction, AI normalisation to practical shopping amounts
- **Household sharing** — Multiple users share recipes, pantry, and meal plans within one household
- **AI recipe import** — Search the web, pick a recipe, and AI catalogues the full ingredients, instructions, and nutrition

## Selling Points

- The only meal planner that calculates different portions for every person in the household
- Portions adjust per day based on training schedule — more on training days, less on rest days
- Shopping list subtracts what you already have — no over-buying, no "didn't I just buy this?"
- Leftovers are tracked and offered for future meals — nothing gets forgotten in the fridge
- Built for phone use in the kitchen — cooking mode is clean, ad-free, and focused

## Positioning

PlateSync is not a diet app or a calorie counter. It's a meal planning tool for families who want to eat well, waste less food, and stop guessing at portions every night. The AI does the maths; you do the cooking.

## Who It's For

- Families with 2+ people eating together regularly
- Parents managing different nutritional needs (adults training, kids growing, dietary restrictions)
- Anyone tracking protein or macros who's tired of calculating portions manually
- Households that waste food from over-buying or forgotten ingredients
