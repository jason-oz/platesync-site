# PlateSync — Marketing

**PlateSync** is a family meal planning app that calculates per-person portions based on each household member's health profile. Smart shopping lists, allergy detection, leftovers tracking, and a mobile cooking guide.

**App:** https://app.getplatesync.com
**Site:** https://getplatesync.com

## Elevator pitch

> "Most meal planners treat your family as one person. PlateSync calculates per-person portions based on each family member's age, weight, and goals — then builds the shopping list around what you already have at home."
